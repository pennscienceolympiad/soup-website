Questions, Comments, Concerns?

We'd love to hear from you! You can contact us through our email at pennscienceolympiad@gmail.com. All emails will be read and replied to by a member of our board within 24 hours. If you have a question about tournament logistics, please first check our FAQ section to see if your question has already been posted. We post major announcements on SciOly.org Forums as well. You can also find us on Facebook and Instagram. Our board will respond to messages through these platforms, especially for questions on the day of the tournament.
