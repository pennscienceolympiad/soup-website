Register for 2020

Registration is now closed! Teams that register after the first 50 teams will be placed on the waitlist.

Payment

Payment must be received by 11 AM Eastern Time on October 31st to secure your spot. If your team is on the waitlist, there is no need to submit payment until we've informed you that we'll be able to grant you a spot in the competition. Registration fees are $70 for each team. Philadelphia school district schools have their registration fees waived.

The preferred payment method is to mail a check to the address below. If you require an alternative method of payment, please contact pennscienceolympiad@gmail.com.

Made out to: Trustees of the University of Pennsylvania
Memo: Science Olympiad at the University of Pennsylvania
Mailing To: Office of Student Affairs at the University of Pennsylvania
Mailing Address: 200 Houston Hall, 3417 Spruce Street, Philadelphia, PA 19104
