Register for 2019

Registration is OPEN. Note that payment must be done separately below

Payment

Payment must be received within 6 weeks of registration date to secure your spot. For teams that preregistered, you have six weeks from the start of registration. If your team is on the waitlist, there is no need to submit payment until we've informed you that we'll be able to grant you a spot in the competition. Registration fees are $45 for each team. Philadelphia school district schools have their registration fees waived.

To pay via credit card, begin by selecting the appropriate number of teams, filling out your team name, and clicking the Pay Now button. This will take you to our PayPal portal, where you can pay either using a credit card or a PayPal account.

Alternatively, you can pay by check with the following information:

Made out to: Trustees of the University of Pennsylvania
Memo: Science Olympiad at the University of Pennsylvania
Mailing To: Office of Student Affairs at the University of Pennsylvania
Mailing Address: 200 Houston Hall, 3417 Spruce Street, Philadelphia, PA 19104
