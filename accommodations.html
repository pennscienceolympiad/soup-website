Things to do

“Go explore the city! Philadelphia is home to both great historical and pop culture sites. Cross the Schuylkill to see the Philadelphia Art Museum and run up the famous Rocky Steps. Check out City Hall and Logan Square. Head down to Old City to see the Liberty Bell, the oldest continuously inhabited street in America, and enjoy a scoop of ice cream at Franklin’s Fountain.
