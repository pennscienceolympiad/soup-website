2021 Tournament

On February 27, 2021, Science Olympiad at Penn hosted its fifth Division C Tournament. 100 teams attended the tournament. Carmel High School (IN) was crowned champion with an overall score of 232. The tournament was hosted virtually through Scilympiad.
